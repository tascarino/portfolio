About

I grew up in Saugerties, NY. I discovered my desire to study Computer Science in my high school's AP CS Principles course. My teacher made the subject fun to learn, and I felt it was something my brain could get a handle on easily.

Despite my interest in the major I chose, I'm not sure where I want to take it, and I'm not sure where I see myself after graduating. My main hope is to find a career with a sustainable work-life balance, leaving some room for myself and my hobbies.

One of the biggest asperations I have for my life outside of a career is to try to make a video game. I'd like to create something that others can enjoy, the same way I've enjoyed the games that passionate artists have put out over the years.
